What is BreadApp?
BreadApp helps you with your home bread baking by storing data about your breads and enabling you to compare the results of your baking.
You can compare factors such as:
  • types and amounts of flours
  • amount of water
  • baking and proofing time
  • baking temperature
In addition it automatically calculates
  • bread hydration
  • total mass of the bread
How does it work?
To add your bread to the database click the "Create your bread" button and follow the form. To create your bread first however you have to have your leaven. You can do it in "Create your leaven" section. You may use leaven recipe created by other users. Upon creating bread and leaven you will be asked to select used flours. If the flour you used is not in our database you can add it by in "View and add flours" section. Finally if you use some fancy flour made of some unusual grains you may also add that type of grain to the database in "View and add grains" section.
